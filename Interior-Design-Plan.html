01

Fall Ceiling
Living & Common Areas

What to build

Three reference styles for the gypsum fall (false) ceiling — pick the one that best suits the living area proportions, or use as a base to adapt.

  • Gypsum board false ceiling with stepped / multi-level profile as shown
  • Recessed LED cove lighting along the perimeter step
  • Leave provision for ceiling fan & main light fitting at center
  • Finish: smooth white / off-white POP finish, ready for paint
02

TV Unit Wall
Living Hall

What to build

Wall-mounted wooden-finish TV console with tall open shelving units flanking either side.

  • Floating wooden-finish TV unit / console, wall-mounted (no legs touching floor)
  • Tall vertical shelving towers on either side for decor, books & plants
  • Built-in warm LED strip lighting inside shelves and under the console
  • Wall behind TV to be finished with wallpaper (textured / fluted panel look as shown)
  • Leave concealed wiring provision for TV power & set-top box behind panel
03

Sofa Back Wall
Living Hall

What to build

Decorative accent treatment for the wall directly behind the living room sofa, facing the TV unit.

  • Match panel design / texture style to the reference image
  • Coordinate finish tone with TV unit wall for a balanced room palette
  • Keep wall fittings minimal — focus on texture & paint/panel finish
04

Dining Ceiling Art
Dining Area

What to build

A decorative ceiling feature above the dining table, distinct from the main living fall ceiling.

  • Ceiling art / design panel centered over the dining table
  • Coordinate with pendant or hanging light fixture above the table
  • Keep proportion in scale with the dining table footprint
05

Kitchen Cabinets & Layout
Kitchen

What to build

Two views of the kitchen — main working wall and the opposite counter — to be read together for full layout.

  • Base cabinets + wall-mounted overhead cabinets, modular style as shown
  • Countertop with sink cutout on main working wall
  • Provision for chimney/hob above cooking counter
  • Storage cabinets on opposite wall for additional kitchen storage / appliances
  • Finish: laminate or similar low-maintenance cabinet finish matching reference tone
06

Pooja Unit
Left of Kitchen

What to build

Compact pooja (prayer) unit positioned in the niche to the left of the kitchen entrance.

  • Wall-mounted or floor-standing pooja unit sized to fit the available niche/wall
  • Wooden finish with carved / textured front panel as shown
  • Small shelf or ledge for idols/lamp; consider a small door or shutter for storage below
  • Coordinate finish with adjacent kitchen & crockery unit
07

Crockery Unit
Near Pooja Area

What to build

Crockery storage unit positioned adjacent to the pooja area, sharing the same wall run.

  • Closed/glass-shutter cabinets for crockery & display items
  • Match cabinet height and finish with the pooja unit for a continuous wall look
  • Mix of open display shelves and closed storage as shown in reference
08

Wardrobe
Master Bedroom

What to build

Full-height built-in wardrobe for the master bedroom, sized to the available wall span.

  • Floor-to-ceiling sliding or hinged shutter wardrobe, finish as per reference
  • Internal layout: hanging space, shelves, and drawer units
  • Handle-less / minimal hardware look if matching reference style
  • Loft storage above wardrobe if ceiling height permits
09

Dressing Table
Master Bedroom

What to build

Dressing table unit, either standalone or integrated alongside the wardrobe wall.

  • Mirror panel with integrated vanity lighting (LED strip / spot lights)
  • Drawer storage below counter for cosmetics/accessories
  • Finish & trim to coordinate with the master wardrobe
10

Wardrobe
Kids Room

What to build

Built-in wardrobe for the kids' room, sized to fit the room's wall layout.

  • Sliding or hinged shutter wardrobe with mixed open/closed compartments
  • Brighter or playful finish tone acceptable, per reference
  • Lower shelves accessible for a child's everyday use
11

Study Unit
Kids Room

What to build

Built-in study desk unit for the kids' room, positioned to use natural light if possible.

  • Wall-mounted or built-in study desk with storage drawers/shelves above or below
  • Provision for table lamp / study light and power socket near desk
  • Finish to coordinate with kids room wardrobe
12

Wash Basin & Storage
Common Area

What to build

Common wash basin counter combined with flanking storage units, for the common/utility area.

  • Counter-mounted wash basin with cabinet storage below
  • Mirror with light fixture above basin
  • Side wardrobe/storage units matched in finish to the basin counter
  • Ensure plumbing provisions (water inlet/outlet) align with counter cutout
